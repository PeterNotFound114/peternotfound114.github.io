About Me

Hello! This is Peter Lu, a chess enthusiast who has played chess since the age of 5. You can start playing a game of chess with me here.

The Netfilx series Queen's Gambit probably sounds familiar to you. It tells the story of a fictional female chess prodigy who defeated not only chess players but also cruel fate and constricting social norms to become the world champion. I strongly recommend watching the show if you have not already done so as not only the plot is cohesive but also the incredible details about chess ( the director consulted Garry Kasparov for games of chess to include in the show ).

Yet, what exactly is the opening Queen's Gambit in chess? This is the question that I try to answer today. Although there will be a table of chess theories about the opening, I also included tips for playing chess and rule of chess in the chess tips page to help you understand better. If you would like to go more in depth of the theories or play a game of chess, feel free to reach out via email or chess.com!
